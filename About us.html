About Us:


RT Welfare and Donation is a dedicated organization committed to saving lives through the power of blood donation. Our mission is to create a supportive and compassionate community of donors who understand the vital role blood donations play in medical care. Founded on the principles of empathy, generosity, and community service, we strive to ensure a steady and safe blood supply for hospitals and clinics.

At RT Welfare and Donation, we believe in making the donation process as seamless and rewarding as possible. Our team of experienced professionals works tirelessly to provide a comfortable and efficient experience for all donors. From the moment you walk in, you are greeted with warmth and gratitude, knowing that your contribution is making a real difference.

We are passionate about educating the public on the importance of blood donation and encouraging more people to become regular donors. By organizing drives, events, and awareness campaigns, we aim to reach out to as many potential donors as possible, fostering a culture of giving and support.

Join us in our mission to save lives and promote health and well-being in our community. With your help, we can continue to provide life-saving blood to those in need and make a positive impact on countless lives. At RT Welfare and Donation, every drop counts, and together, we can create a brighter, healthier future for everyone.
